Persian Rice Recipe

Home

Description

Let me introduce you to a delicious Persian rice dish made with golden potatoes and saffron

Ingredients

  • 3 quarts water
  • 3 tablespoons Kosher salt
  • 2 cups basmati rice, rinsed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 Russet potato, cut into 1/4-inch slices
  • 1 pinch ground cumin
  • salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons butter, cut into thin slices, or to taste
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place water and kosher salt in a pot; bring to a boil. Add rice; cook and stir for exactly 7 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pot over medium-high heat. Cover bottom of the pot with 1 layer of potato slices. Cook until potatoes are sizzling, 2 to 3 minutes; top potatoes with rice to form an even layer. Reduce heat to low and place butter slices over rice.
  3. Top the pot with a clean layer of paper towels, then place the lid over the towels. Steam until rice is fluffy, about 45 minutes.
  4. Grind saffron threads with a mortar and pestle. Mix crushed saffron with 2 tablespoons hot water in a large bowl. Add a couple of spoonfuls of rice to saffron mixture and stir until rice is yellow.
  5. Spoon remaining rice into a serving bowl. Top with saffron rice and line the edges of the bowl with potatoes. Garnish with parsley.
